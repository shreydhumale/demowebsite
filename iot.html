About IOT

The Internet of things (IoT) is a system of interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, animals or people that are provided with unique identifiers ( UIDs ) and the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction. IoT is a sensor network of billions of smart devices that connect people, systems and other applications to collect and share data.
