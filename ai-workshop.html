Conducted workshop on AI & Robotics

Published: July 21, 2023 | By ESRO IT Team

The ESRO IT founder Mr. Ashish Kandalkar conducted an intensive workshop on AI & Robotics at the Pune Technical Institute, attended by over 120 students and faculty members.


Increasing unemployment is the biggest problem in front of the country, but even a technically proficient student can stand on his own feet on the strength of his skills and give employment to others, so students should become computer friendly and get complete knowledge. ISRO company's director Ashish Kandalkar said this. Robotics and coding workshop was organized for students at Swayam Computer and Academy by Swayam Multipurpose Foundation in Yevda, Tehsil. On this occasion, Ashish Kandalkar informed the students about the latest information on computer, artificial intelligence, information and technology in the field of Information and Technology. Students should become computer friendly Ashish Kandalkar's statement and new challenges, how students can update themselves. Can give information about how to get commands on the computer. At the beginning of the program, Sanjay Waghmare, the guide and former group education officer of Multipurpose Foundation himself, Principal Trimbak Sontakke, Prashant Hadole, competitive exam instructor Dnyaneshwar Meshram etc. welcomed him. Motivational speakers Ashish Prajapati, Pawan Aapkaje gave students personality development, education system. Informed about the changes, various programs and activities being run by them to enhance the talent of the students. Nakul Sontakke, Rohit Gawande, Akshay Baiskar, Shyam Gawande, Vikrant Dhawale, Dhammasheel Meshram, Gyanpal Raut etc. made efforts for the success of the workshop.
